# Mainfranken Community Connect — Pitch Script

**AI Vibe Hackathon #4 · Challenge: "Mainfranken Community Connect" · Sponsor: IT-Verband Mainfranken**

> Format: 7 slides, ~3 minutes. Roughly 25 seconds per slide, leaving ~60s for the live demo (Slide 4). Talk fast, point at the screen, don't read the slides.

---

## Slide 1 — THE PROBLEM

### Headline: "One of Bavaria's most active tech regions. An empty events page."

**Talking points:**
- IT-Mainfranken is a powerhouse — but go to their site right now, open the events tab... it's empty.
- People hear about events *after* they happen. Discovery is broken.
- How did *we* find this hackathon? A friend called us. He found it in a random Discord.
- That's the whole problem in one sentence: the best events spread by accident.

> **Stage direction:** Open the slide on the real, empty `it-mainfranken.org/it-events/` tab in a browser tab. Let the silence land for a beat before you start.

---

## Slide 2 — THE INSIGHT

### Headline: "Discovery isn't a database problem. It's a people problem."

**Talking points:**
- People trust people, not platforms. Every event we attend, someone told us about.
- The organizers handed us four questions: How do people **find** events? Why do people **share**? How are people **connected**? How will people **know**?
- Every feature we built answers one of those four. Hold that frame.

> **Stage direction:** Put the four questions on screen as four short bullets. Tap each one as you say it.

---

## Slide 3 — OUR SOLUTION

### Headline: "Two moving parts: AI fills the catalog, people fill the room."

**Talking points:**
- **One — AI-powered ingestion.** Paste a URL. AI extracts the structured event data. The empty tab fills itself. Zero manual entry for organizers.
- **Two — verified referrals.** Every attendee becomes a growth channel. They share, friends come.
- The catch that makes it real: points only fully count when the referred person *physically checks in* via QR. No voucher hunters, no fake signups.

> **Stage direction:** Two big icons — a magic wand and a chain link. Resist explaining the tech; you're about to show it.

---

## Slide 4 — HOW IT WORKS (LIVE DEMO)

### Headline: "Let me show you the whole loop in 60 seconds."

**Talking points (narrate while clicking):**
- **Organizer:** paste an event URL → AI fills the form → event is live in the catalog.
- **Attendee:** register → get a unique referral link → share to WhatsApp / Telegram / X / LinkedIn with a pre-written "I'm attending X — join me!"
- **Friend:** opens the link → registers → gets a QR ticket.
- **At the door:** organizer scans the QR → check-in confirmed → the referrer's reward ticks up. Then a nudge points them to Discord & LinkedIn.

> **Stage direction:** SWITCH TO LIVE APP NOW. Drive the real flow: paste → extract → register → copy link → scan QR → see check-in confirm. If the demo gods are angry, fall back to a pre-recorded GIF. Keep narrating; don't go quiet while pages load.

---

## Slide 5 — WHAT ORGANIZERS GET

### Headline: "The dashboard organizers have never had."

**Talking points:**
- **Real referral analytics:** who shared, how many signups, and how many *actually showed up* — per referrer.
- **Configurable rewards:** set a threshold (5 check-ins = a hoodie), pick signup mode or check-in mode.
- **Channel truth:** which platforms drive real attendance, not just clicks.
- And it costs them zero extra work to post an event — the AI did that on Slide 4.

> **Stage direction:** Show the organizer event-detail page with the referral table and stat cards visible. Point at the "check-ins driven" column.

---

## Slide 6 — SOCIAL PROOF & CONNECTION

### Headline: "The community doesn't end when the event does."

**Talking points:**
- Every event card shows **"N people going"** with attendee avatars — social proof you can see before you commit.
- Each event has a **referral leaderboard** surfacing the top community ambassadors.
- After check-in, a nudge says: join the Discord, follow on LinkedIn, browse more events.
- That's question three answered: people stay *connected*, not just informed.

> **Stage direction:** Flash the event card with avatars, then the leaderboard, then the post-check-in nudge — three quick beats.

---

## Slide 7 — ROADMAP & CLOSE

### Headline: "We filled the tab. Next we grow the graph."

**Talking points:**
- Automated multi-source scrapers — Luma, Meetup, university calendars.
- "People you follow are going" — a real social graph.
- Deeper channel analytics: which platform drives the most *check-ins*.
- Organizer-to-ambassador outreach tools, and a mobile-first PWA.

**Closing line:**
> "IT-Mainfranken's events tab is empty today. With Mainfranken Community Connect, it fills itself — and every attendee brings the next one. Discovery, made social."

> **Stage direction:** Land the closing line looking at the judges, not the screen. Then stop talking. Thank you.
